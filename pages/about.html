About

Friendly local IT with enterprise-level discipline

Lazy Dog Computing was built to give small and midsize businesses access to strong Microsoft, security, backup, and management practices without losing the personal service of a local provider.

Technology should reduce stress, not create more of it

Our work centers on understanding how a business operates, identifying what creates risk or frustration, and implementing improvements that are sustainable. We value clear communication, documentation, security, and practical decision-making.

Our primary technology stack includes Microsoft 365, Intune, Defender, Entra ID, NinjaOne, Veeam, Tenable, Nessus, Ubiquiti, and Jamf Pro where appropriate.

What clients can expect

  • Clear explanations
  • Recommendations tied to business needs
  • Security and reliability as default priorities
  • Respect for budget and operational limits
  • A local relationship rather than anonymous support

Start with a practical conversation

Tell us what is working, what is frustrating your team, and what you are concerned about. We will help you identify a sensible next step.
